Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

This church was built by the school authority to give an opportunity to the children to pray God in the school. It increases religious soul in the children. They also get the chance to pray with classmates,friends and teachers. It has been maintained since that time and is cleaned every week.



School Library

Was built in 1980

This is the main Library. Here there are many books such as stories of cartoons,panchtantra, fairy tales, mythlogy, etc. for each standard or grade. There are novels too. There are books of math, science, gk, english, german, sanskrit, french, computers and Hindi grade-wise . There are books such as R. S. Agrawal, Maths step, Pearson, etc. for practice of competitive exams. There are some books of unbelivable facts and G.K. mysteries. There is atlas, history, politics, biographies and autobiographies for social knowledge.There is workbooks, puzzles and comic for fun reading.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

This is the Auditorium. Here all the assemblies and special meeting are taken. Here the competitions of district and state levels are taken. It is very big. Certain representations are also held. It is neat and clean. There are more than a thousand seats in the auditorium. Here all the programs are held and awards are also distributed here.
